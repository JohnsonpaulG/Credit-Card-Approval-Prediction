How It Works

From application to decision in four steps

Here's exactly what happens behind the scenes between the moment you submit your details and the moment you see APPROVED or REJECTED on screen.

01

Applicant Data

You fill in a guided, four-part form covering your personal, income and employment details. Nothing is pre-filled or assumed.

02

Encoding & Scaling

Every text field (like "Married" or "Working") is converted to the exact numeric code the model was trained on, then scaled the same way the training data was.

03

Model Scoring

A Random Forest classifier — selected after comparing four algorithms on real historical data — scores your application and produces a probability.

04

Instant Decision

You immediately see an APPROVED or REJECTED verdict, a confidence score, and a full summary of the details you submitted.

Under the Hood

What the model actually looks at

Personal Profile

Gender, age, family status, family size and number of children.

Financial Standing

Annual income, income type, and whether you own a car or property.

Employment History

Occupation, years employed, education level and housing type.
